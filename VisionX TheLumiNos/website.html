Project Blueprint

Explore the technical foundation, key achievements, and strategic limitations of the Civic Connect AI platform. Use the tabs below to navigate through the project's core components.

  • Core AI Model:OpenAI GPT-4.0 for categorization and authenticity validation.
  • Frontend:React for responsive citizen and government dashboards.
  • Backend:Node.js with Express for API management.
  • Database:MySQL deployed on a cloud platform for high availability.
  • **Improved Public Health:** Expected significant reduction in preventable diseases like dengue by resolving issues like water blockages.
  • **Reduced Bureaucratic Friction:** Drastically cuts down miscommunication and delays, promoting responsive governance.
  • **Enhanced Citizen Trust:** The reward mechanism motivates exponential growth in community involvement and reinforces a sustainable cycle of civic engagement.
  • **High Operating Costs:** Reliance on external APIs (GPT-4.0) and cloud hosting incurs significant recurring fees.
  • **Mapping Precision:** Uses OpenStreetMap for cost reasons, which is slightly less precise than commercial alternatives.
  • **Internet Reliability:** The system is critically dependent on stable internet, posing a risk in regions with connectivity issues.
